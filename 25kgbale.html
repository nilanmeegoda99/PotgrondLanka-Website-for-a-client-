25 Kg Bale

The farmers who need in bulk quantities of coir fiber pith/ coco husk chips we produce it as bulk bag (25kg approx.). These raw material especially ideal for hydroponic and horticulture application in preparing growing media as well as a soil conditioner. There for we use various size of husk chips, as examples, 3-6mm / 6-12mm / 12-18mm / < 12mm / < 15mm. In additionally these economical bulk bag can be used for animal bedding, potting mixes and for landscaping purposes too.

According to the customer’s choice , we offer CaNO3 treated /washed and unwashed coir fiber pith and coco husk chips.
