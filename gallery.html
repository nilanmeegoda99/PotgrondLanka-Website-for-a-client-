Our PotGround Lanka Products for Different Crops & Growths

5 Kg Bale

Cocopeat / Coco peat can be pressed in diverse forms after drying. One of the compressed forms is 5 kg cocopeat bale. It has the moisture capacity below 25%.This product is available washed, not washed or buffered material and delivery possible on pallets. Size, Packaging, Quality can be customizable according to buyer's request.

Grow Bags

We produce coir fiber pith grow bags are with various raw material mixtures & sizes suit to the different type of Crops to get the excellent results and excellent yield. Especially we have focus on Tomato, Cucumber, Cabbage, Zucchini, Strawberry , Gerbera, Roses and so on with using Calcium Nitrate treated coir fiber pith , Untreated or well washed coir fiber pith.

Planter Bags

Cocopeat planter bags are specially designed bags with a compressed coco peat and husk chips materials. Ideal for tomatoes, strawberry, bell peppers.
Planter Bags sometimes called as Easy Fill Bag or Open Top Bag another popular Mediums for Green House soilless growing system. Popularly used in Green houses for Vegetable such as tomatoes, cucumbers and capsicums . They can be produced in 100% Peat, 100% Hydro (Coco chips) or both Mixed. This saves cost in watering and reduces the risk of contamination.

25Kg Bale

The farmers who need in bulk quantities of coir fiber pith/ coco husk chips we produce it as bulk bag (25kg approx.). These raw material especially ideal for hydroponic and horticulture application in preparing growing media as well as a soil conditioner. There for we use various size of husk chips, as examples, 3-6mm / 6-12mm / 12-18mm / < 12mm / < 15mm. In additionally these economical bulk bag can be used for animal bedding, potting mixes and for landscaping purposes too.

Gerbera Discs


Quality compressed Cocopeat disk which has been specially treated to give excellent results for a variety of potted plants Ideal for home and commercial hydroponics. Each Cocopeat disk fills a one-liter pot when re-hydrated. Size, Packaging, Quality can be customizable in according to customer request.

650g Bricks

Easy grow lanka briquettes are made with either cocopith or Husk chips according to customer requirements. This product is usually made for home gardening purposes as well as Domestic. Material can be blended with using N:P:K fertilizer or without fertilizer as per to buyers requirement. Packaging can be done with naked form in cartoons or shrink wrapped with label.

Propergation Cubes

Grow easy propagation cubes, This can be widely used for seeding and seeds germination in hydrophonies and horticulture. This is a new concept forthe replacement of traditional rock wool on pent moss blocks. Plug hole (Pre Cut Hole) propagation cube mostly used for the growing stages of the plants.
